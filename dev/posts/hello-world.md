Social media is poison, tech is enshittified, DEI is a slur, there's no place left for me in this world. So what am I to do? Go back to the Web I feel comfortable with. The Web that is still out there, [if you look hard enough](https://neocities.org).

This is the first site in a long time I'm using simply vanilla CSS and JavaScript. Since the Web just gave up on HTML templating after a [botched attempt in the 90s](https://harrypujols.com/framesets/), I am using the Liquid Templating language with a [static site generator](https://www.11ty.dev).

The last time I kept a blog was 20 years ago, it gradually died on the early days of social media. Let's see how long this one will last.
